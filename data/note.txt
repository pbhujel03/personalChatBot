Objectives: 
The main objectives of this internship are: 
• To gain hands-on experience in WordPress development. 
• To learn theme customization and plugin integration. 
• To build responsive and user-friendly websites. 
• To understand professional workflows at a digital agency. 
• To apply theoretical knowledge in real projects. 
  
 
6 
 
Description of the Internship Work (Expected): 
1. WordPress Fundamentals & Setup:  
I will learn WordPress architecture, database structure, and install/configure it on 
local and hosting environments. 
2. Theme Development & Customization:  
I will customize themes, work with child themes, and ensure responsive layouts. 
3. Page Builders & Visual Design:  
I will design pages using Elementor, Gutenberg, or WPBakery and maintain 
consistent branding. 
4. Plugin Integration & Management:  
I will install, configure, and manage plugins for SEO, forms, security, and social 
media. 
5. Custom Development & Coding:  
I will write HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and PHP for custom features and 
functionalities. 
6. Website Optimization & Performance:  
I will optimize website speed, caching, images, and implement SEO best 
practices. 
7. Testing, Debugging & QA:  
I will test websites on different devices and browsers, fix bugs, and ensure proper 
functionality. 
8. Security & Maintenance:  
I will implement security measures, perform updates, backups, and monitor 
website health. 
9. Client Collaboration & Project Management:  
I will participate in meetings, understand client needs, and manage tasks using 
project tools.
